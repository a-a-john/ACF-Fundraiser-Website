About Hope in Motion

We started as a few volunteers at the Arthur J. E. Child Comprehensive Cancer Centre passionate about improving cancer experiences for Albertans. Now we're a Calgary-based team, comprised of volunteers, (family and friends?) running to raise funds for the Alberta Cancer Foundation.

Track our progress as we aim to run 900 km from August 15th to October 28st. October 28st marks the one-year anniversary of the opening of the Arthur Child Centre, so help us give back to celebrate this special day!

If you'd like to donate to the Alberta Cancer Foundation in our name and help us reach our goal, please visit the donation link below.
